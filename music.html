Sandeep, the violinist

Extracurricular activities also formed an important aspect of the academic curriculum of Raffles Instituition due to which he was encouraged to take up the violinwhile he was in Singapore. After relocating to Chennai, under the tutelage of emminent Carnatic musician Vidwan Shri R.K.Shriramkumar, Sandeep has been learning to play classical music on his violin for 14 years now. And he will continue to do so, as Music never ebbs away.
