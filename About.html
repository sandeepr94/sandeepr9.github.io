About Sandeep

Passionate Violinist. MBA Aspirant. Avid Football Fan.

While donning these hats, Sandeep completed his Bachelors in Engineering in Electronics and Communication Engineering from SSN one of Tamil Nadu's premier instituitions. An outstanding classical violinist, he often finds himself playing for the Who's Who of the music fraternity. Smitten by his Rubik's cube and brain teasers, he keeps solving puzzles. Besides bowing away on his violin or checking his phone for football updates, he enjoys pristine English and a cup of coffee.
